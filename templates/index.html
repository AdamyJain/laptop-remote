Free & open source

Control your laptop
from your phone.

Laptop Remote turns your phone into a wireless trackpad and keyboard. No cables, no pairing — just download the client, run it, and you're ready.

Download for Windows Download for Mac
How it works
1
Download & run the client on your laptop
Double-click the downloaded file. It runs in the background — no installation needed.
2
Copy the URL that opens in your browser
The client automatically opens a setup page with a unique URL for your session. Hit Copy URL.
3
Open the URL on your phone
Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network. Once you open it, your phone instantly becomes a trackpad and keyboard.
